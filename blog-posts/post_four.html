January 20th, 2023

11:09:58 A.M

My friend described the terror of getting mugged at a music festival. She was alone, her phone and wallet gone. Just as she was about to despair, a kind stranger appeared, giving her a bottle of water and a phone to call a friend.

The stranger stayed with her, even offering to walk her to her car. My friend was struck by the kindness of this stranger, and it gave her hope that there were still good people in the world.
