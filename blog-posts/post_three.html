To capture candid photos of people, I rely on a simple trick - I keep my camera at my waist level and shoot with a wide angle lens. This way, I'm able to capture the scene without having to point the camera directly at the person.

Additionally, I keep my camera settings pre-set and use silent shooting mode to avoid any noise, which could alarm or distract someone.

Lastly, I make sure I'm in the right place at the right time to capitalize on any spontaneous situations that arise. It takes patience and practice, but when you master the art of taking candid photos, the results are worth the effort.
